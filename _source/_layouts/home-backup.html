With expertise in both the voice over and video game industries, we blend our skills together to provide personalized services to game development studios and help them cast their video games from start to finish.

Our services

For video game development studios we offer voice casting services, production support and partner with studios to cast their games in schedule with production. From holding casting calls and auditions to signing and finalising voice talent in your projects. We also work with independent voice over artists: we will help you find your next great role and put you on track with your journey, giving practical advice and actionable steps to take your career to the next level.
